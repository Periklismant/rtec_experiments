HasTime

trait HasTime[Time] extends AnyRef

HasTime is a trait for flagging that its instances have a time field of type Time. It is typically used for literals and other logical expressions. Instances of Timed are implicitly partially ordered by LaterOrderingForTimed in the natural way.
